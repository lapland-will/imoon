iTable

Diners get to control every step of their meal, using the interactive touch-screen table. When you’re ready to order, photos of the dishes on the menu get projected onto the screen, and once you’ve placed your food and drink order you can play a game or even watch the chef prepare your meal via webcam. When it’s time to head home, patrons can pay through the table and can even order a taxi to drive them home.
